About Us:

After being part of the keto lifestyle for a number of years, we grew frustrated with a lack of good options to properly measure ketone levels. The limited amount of meters on the market were either very expensive to try, overly complicated to use or gave inconsistent readings. We set out to find a meter that addresses those issues and are happy to bring a product to market that is affordable, easy to use right out of the box and gives accurate readings down to 0.1 mmol.
